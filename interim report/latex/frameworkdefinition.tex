\begin{defn} an attack is defined as follows:
\newline

An argument $A_1 \vdash \sigma_1$ attacks an argument $A_2 \vdash \sigma_2$ iff $\sigma_1$ is the contrary of one of the assumptions in $A_2$.

\end{defn}
